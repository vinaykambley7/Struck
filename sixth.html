Opertaion Meghdoot

Operation Meghdoot was the codename for the Indian Armed Forces.

Operation to take full control of the Siachen Glacier in Ladakh.

  1. Executed on the morning of 13 April 1984 in the highest battlefield in the world, Meghdoot was the first military offensive of its kind.
  • Date : 13 April 1984; 40 years ago
  • Location : Siachen Glacier, Ladakh
  • Result : Indian Victory
  • Territorial changes : India gains control of the entire Siachen Glacier, and administers it as part of Ladakh

This operation preempted Pakistan's Operation Ababeel and was a success, resulting in Indian forces gaining control of the Siachen Glacier in its entirety.

  • Operation Meghdoot was led by Lieutenant General Prem Nath Hoon
